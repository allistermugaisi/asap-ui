Asap Intell MSME platform

The MSME sector in Kenya has over the years been recognized for its role in the provision of goods and services, enhancing competition, fostering innovation, generating employment and in effect, alleviation of poverty. MSME contribute 28.5 percent of the total economy in Kenya.

The MSME sector has been identified and prioritized as a key growth driver for achievement of the development blue print for vision 2030.The MSMEs have not fully been exploited their potential due to lack of data information, ASAP Intell MSME PLATFORM will be able to utilize its full capacity by sourcing enough business information, verify and analyze it for better synthesis to potential financial institutions for either funding or borrowing. The manufacturing sector will a better picture on MSMEs which will enhance smooth trade credit procedures to make better informed trade credit decisions.

ASAP Intell MSME PLATFORM’s objective is also aligned with the Vision 2030 objectives by compiling comprehensive data, at national and county levels, on the characteristics, operations, dynamics and evolving nature of all businesses in the country.
